MODULE 4 INTRODUCTION TO LOAD BALANCER AND AUTOSCALING

1.LOAD BALANCER IS A SERVICE THAT UNIFORMLY DISTRIBUTES THE NETWORK TRAFFICE AND WORKLOADS ACROSS MULTIPLE SERVERS OR A CLUSTER OF SERVER.
2.LOAD BALANCER INCREASES THE AVAILABILITY AND FAULT TOLERANCE OF AN APPLICATION.
3.ELASTIC LOAD BALANCER IS A LOAD BALANCING SERVICE FOR AWS DEPLOYMENTS
4.ELB SCALES ITSELF AS NECESSARY TO HANDLE THE LOAD.
5.INCOMING TRAFFIC IS DISTRIBUTED ACROSS EC2 INSTANCES IN MULTIPLE AVAILABILITY ZONES.
6.LOAD BALANCER IS THE SINGLE POINT OF CONTACT FOR CLIENTS.
